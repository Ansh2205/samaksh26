Two Flagship Events

Choose your battlefield. Will you dominate the player market in the high-stakes auction, or will you shape the nation's economic future in the parliament?

Event 01

IPL Auction

The ultimate valuation challenge. Build the most value-optimized cricket squad within a rigid budget. Master real-time valuation, risk management, and competitor analysis under high-pressure "hammer" conditions.

Game Objectives

  • Construct a roster of 16-18 players.
  • Maximize overall squad performance ratings.
  • Strictly adhere to the ₹140 Crore virtual purse limit.

The Bidding Cycle

  • 1.Announcement: Profile & base price revealed.
  • 2.Live Bids: "Place Bid" on local interfaces.
  • 4.Closing: Hammer drops when bids stop.
Event 02

Finance Parliament

Policy Debate

Rules & Regulations

Please review the following mandates carefully. Any violation may result in immediate disqualification from Samaksh 2026.

IPL Mock Auction

1

Budget Strictness

Teams must strictly adhere to the ₹140 Cr virtual purse. Server-side validation will block any over-budget bids instantly.

2

Squad Limits

Each franchise must secure a minimum of 16 players and a maximum of 18 players to successfully qualify their team.

3

Single Device

Only the designated primary laptop can be used for placing bids and accessing the centralized system dashboard.

4

Auto Increments

Bids auto-increment by 5L up to 2 Cr, and by 20L for values above 5 Cr. Custom bid amounts are not permitted.

Finance Parliament

1

Decorum

Parliamentary decorum must be maintained during debates. Unsportsmanlike behavior will attract heavy point penalties.

2

Time Allocation

Strict speaking time limits will be enforced. Microphones will be muted automatically once the allotted time expires.

3

Bill Drafting

All economic policies and bills must adhere to the standardized formats provided by the organizing committee.

4

Final Authority

The decisions made by the Speaker of the House regarding the passing of bills are absolute and binding.
